TamaJakey

Virtual pet

The year is 2023, the United-States government began planning the creation of the perfect Jakey. Their efforts were stopped during the beginning of the Great Jakey War. 50 years after the end of the war, you stumble upon the ruins of a government science lab. Inide you find a V.01 Jakey left abondoned.

You are tasked to take care of Jakey!

Jakey only requires a few things to survive, but it is important to keep him happy.

Keep Jakey Clean, Fed, and Happy and you will have a life long pal!! If his stats run out you lose! If hes kept happy until the timer runs out, you win!
